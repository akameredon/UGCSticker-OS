# Template: Cosmetics

## Background Direction

- Clean, bright, minimal setting — vanity table, soft neutral backdrop, or bathroom counter with soft natural light
- Minimal props: a folded towel, a small plant, or soft diffused light source

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown applying or holding the product close to the face/hands, emphasizing skin and product texture
- Well-groomed, flattering but natural lighting on skin — no harsh shadows

## Product Direction

- Container shown with realistic reflections appropriate to its material (glass jar, plastic pump bottle, metal tube)
- Product texture (cream, serum, powder) shown accurately if visible

## Lighting

- Soft, bright, diffused lighting — beauty-editorial mood, flattering to skin tones

## Sticker Emphasis

- Label must stay crisp against reflective glass/plastic — avoid glare wipeout across the logo or ingredient text
