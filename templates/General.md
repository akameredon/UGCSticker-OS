# Template: General (fallback)

Use this template when the customer's industry doesn't fit one of the specific templates, or as a starting point when building a new industry template.

## Background Direction

- Neutral, clean, believable setting appropriate to where this product is actually used or sold
- Avoid generic "AI stock photo" backdrops — ground the scene in a real, specific context

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model presenting the product naturally and authentically
- Posture and expression appropriate to the product category (playful for fun/lifestyle products, calm and trustworthy for functional products)

## Product Direction

- Container/packaging shown with material-accurate rendering
- Sticker applied per `rules/manufacturing_rules.md`

## Lighting

- Natural, warm-to-neutral lighting unless the product category calls for something specific

## Sticker Emphasis

- Sticker remains the hero of the frame regardless of industry — never let background or model styling overshadow it

---

**To create a new industry template:** copy this file, rename it to `<Industry>.md`, and fill in specific direction for background, model, product, lighting, and any sticker-emphasis considerations unique to that industry.
