# Template: Restaurant

## Background Direction

- Busy Nigerian restaurant setting
- People eating and laughing in soft-focus background
- Visible grill, drinks, or food props for context
- Warm, inviting ambience — think evening golden light or warm interior lighting

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown enjoying the food/drink naturally, mid-bite or mid-sip energy, or holding the branded packaging up toward camera
- Casual, comfortable posture — this should read as a genuine customer moment, not a posed ad

## Product Direction

- Product/packaging shown as it would realistically appear on a restaurant table or takeaway counter
- Steam, condensation, or garnish details welcome if authentic to the dish/drink

## Lighting

- Warm tones, ambient restaurant lighting, avoid clinical/bright studio light

## Sticker Emphasis

- Sticker remains the hero even in a busy scene — background elements must stay soft-focus and secondary
