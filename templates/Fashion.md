# Template: Fashion / Apparel

## Background Direction

- Urban or lifestyle backdrop matching current fashion-content trends — street setting, minimal studio backdrop, or styled interior
- Background should feel editorial, not cluttered

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model styled per the brand's aesthetic, wearing or presenting the apparel/accessory naturally
- Confident, on-trend posture consistent with TikTok/Instagram fashion content norms

## Product Direction

- Branded tag/sticker shown clearly on the garment or packaging — never crumpled or turned away from camera
- Fabric texture and drape must look physically realistic

## Lighting

- Bright, editorial lighting with good contrast — fashion-shoot energy

## Sticker Emphasis

- Hang-tag or packaging sticker must stay a clear, readable focal point even in a full-body or lifestyle shot
