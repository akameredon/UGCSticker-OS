# Template: Juice / Beverage

## Background Direction

- Bright, fresh, energetic setting — kitchen counter, outdoor daylight, or fruit-market backdrop matching the flavor
- Fresh fruit props matching the juice flavor (visible but not overpowering the product)

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown mid-sip, or holding the bottle up toward camera with a refreshed, satisfied expression
- Natural condensation droplets on the bottle for a "cold and fresh" feel

## Product Direction

- Bottle/container shown with realistic liquid color and fill level matching the actual product
- Condensation and light refraction through liquid must be physically accurate to a chilled beverage

## Lighting

- Bright, crisp, natural daylight feel — high energy, high clarity

## Sticker Emphasis

- Label must remain crisp and legible even with condensation on the bottle — never let droplets obscure text or logo
