# Template: Pharmaceutical / Health

## Background Direction

- Clean, clinical, trustworthy setting — pharmacy counter, home medicine shelf, or plain neutral backdrop
- Minimal props; avoid anything that looks staged or gimmicky given the sensitive category

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown holding or presenting the product respectfully and clearly — no exaggerated claims implied by expression or gesture
- Calm, trustworthy demeanor

## Product Direction

- Packaging/label shown with full accuracy — this category has zero tolerance for altered text, dosage information, or warnings
- Blister packs, bottles, or boxes rendered with correct material and print finish

## Lighting

- Bright, even, clinical lighting — clarity and trust over mood

## Sticker Emphasis

- All regulatory/label text must remain perfectly legible and completely unaltered — this template has the strictest fidelity requirement in the framework
