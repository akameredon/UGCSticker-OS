# Template: Household Products

## Background Direction

- Clean home setting relevant to the product's use case — kitchen, laundry area, or bathroom
- Everyday, relatable environment — this is about practical trust, not aspiration

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown using the product in a natural household task (cleaning a surface, doing laundry, etc.)
- Practical, competent, approachable energy

## Product Direction

- Container shown accurately for its real material (plastic bottle, spray trigger, box, sachet)
- Any liquid/product color visible through packaging must be accurate

## Lighting

- Bright, clean, everyday indoor lighting

## Sticker Emphasis

- Instructions/branding sticker must remain legible even with a spray trigger, cap, or handle partially in frame
