# Template: Packaged Food

## Background Direction

- Kitchen counter, pantry shelf, or market-stall setting depending on product type
- Complementary ingredients visible in soft focus (e.g. spices around a spice-blend package)

## Model Direction

- Approved UGC model shown using the product in a cooking/prep moment, or presenting the pack directly to camera
- Natural, homely energy — this is about trust and everyday use, not high glamour

## Product Direction

- Packaging shown with realistic material behavior — flexible pouch, rigid box, jar, etc.
- Any transparency (e.g. a window on a pouch) must show the real product inside accurately

## Lighting

- Warm, appetizing light — avoid cold/clinical tones

## Sticker Emphasis

- Nutritional/branding sticker text must remain fully legible even on non-flat or flexible packaging
