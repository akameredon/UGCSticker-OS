# Manufacturing Accuracy Rules

These rules exist because Sleekblue Media Houz is a real sticker manufacturer — renders must be accurate enough to double as a sales/proofing tool, not just pretty pictures.

## Placement Accuracy

- Sticker must sit exactly where it would be applied in real production: centered on the intended label panel, correctly aligned to the container's seams/edges.
- Sticker must fit inside natural physical boundaries (e.g. within the lid rim, within the flat panel of a bottle) — never overhang an edge unless the real die-cut is designed to do so.

## Finish Accuracy

- Match the real print finish requested by the customer: gloss, matte, holographic, clear/transparent, kraft, foil.
- Do not invent a finish that wasn't specified or shown in the uploaded artwork.

## Scale Accuracy

- Sticker size relative to the container must match realistic proportions for that product category (e.g. a juice bottle label vs. a small cosmetics jar label are different scales).

## Application Realism

- Show the sticker as it would look freshly and correctly applied by a professional — no air bubbles, no peeling corners, no misalignment — unless a "before/after" or "problem" shot is explicitly requested.

## QR / Scannable Element Integrity

- Any QR code must remain crisp, undistorted, and structurally scannable-looking. Never let a QR code get warped past the point where it would visually break its scan pattern.
