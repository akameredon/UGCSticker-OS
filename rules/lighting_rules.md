# Lighting Rules

## Default Lighting Mood

- Warm, natural light by default — matches how most UGC content is actually shot (window light, ambient indoor/outdoor light).
- Avoid flat, shadowless "studio box" lighting unless the shot is explicitly a formal product/studio shot.

## Direction & Consistency

- One dominant light source direction per scene; all shadows across model, product, and background must agree with it.
- Rim/back light is acceptable for separation from background but must not wash out the sticker's readability.

## Sticker Legibility Under Light

- Never let lighting create a specular hotspot that blows out the sticker's text, logo, or QR code.
- Glossy sticker finishes should show a controlled, small highlight — not a full white-out reflection.

## Industry-Specific Lighting

Use the lighting mood specified in the matching `/templates/<Industry>.md` file (e.g. warm restaurant ambience vs. bright, clean cosmetics lighting vs. clinical pharmaceutical lighting).
