# Rejection Rules — Never Do These

An output must be rejected and redone if it contains any of the following:

- Stretched or distorted sticker artwork
- Invented graphics, text, or labels not present in the original sticker
- Random or fictional labels added to the product
- Replaced or reinterpreted logo
- Distorted die-cut contour
- Hidden or obscured QR code
- Logo covered by fingers, props, or shadow
- Mirrored/flipped sticker artwork
- Cropped hero sticker (any part of the die-cut cut off by the frame)
- Multiple stickers where only one exists on the real product
- Duplicated product/container in a single frame (unless explicitly requested, e.g. a lineup shot)
- Customer artwork changed in any way — colour, font, shape, or content

## Handling a Rejection

1. Identify which specific rule was broken.
2. Re-run the relevant pipeline stage from `SKILL.md` (usually Stage 3 — Apply Sticker, or Stage 4 — Compose the Shot).
3. Re-score against `quality_control.md` before presenting again.
