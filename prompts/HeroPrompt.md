# Prompt: Hero Shot

**Use for:** primary product hero image — the main "face" shot of the campaign.

## Direction

Generate a hero shot where the approved UGC model holds the customer's product with the sticker fully visible, facing the camera, occupying roughly 40% of the frame per the Hero Rule in `rules/composition_rules.md`. The container occupies roughly 20% of the frame per the Container Rule. Background follows the matching industry template.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Sticker fully visible, uncropped, facing camera
- [ ] Every rule in `rules/sticker_rules.md` obeyed exactly
- [ ] Passes every item in `rules/quality_control.md`
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 4:5 (see `rules/output_rules.md`)
- [ ] Output meets the bar in `rules/output_rules.md`

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A high-end commercial hero photograph. [Approved UGC model description] holds [product name]
toward the camera, the die-cut sticker fully visible and perfectly readable, occupying
approximately 40% of the frame. The product/container occupies approximately 20% of the frame,
sticker applied naturally with realistic reflections and shadows. Background: [insert from
matching industry template]. Lighting: [insert from rules/lighting_rules.md and the industry
template]. Ultra photorealistic, 8K, commercial advertising quality, natural skin, correct
material physics, ready for e-commerce and social use.
```
