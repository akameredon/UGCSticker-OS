# Prompt: Lifestyle Shot

**Use for:** in-context, storytelling imagery showing the product being used/enjoyed naturally (social feed, website banners).

## Direction

Generate a candid-feeling lifestyle shot of the approved UGC model using or enjoying the product within the matching industry template's background. The product and sticker remain identifiable but the framing is more relaxed/environmental than the Hero shot — this is about mood and authenticity.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Sticker still legible even though it's not the dominant frame element
- [ ] Scene believable for the real-world context of product use (see industry template)
- [ ] Passes `rules/quality_control.md`
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 4:5 or 16:9

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A candid, editorial-style lifestyle photograph. [Approved UGC model description] naturally
[using/enjoying/presenting] [product name] within [insert setting from matching industry
template]. The sticker on the product remains legible: [insert locked sticker analysis —
shape, colours, typography, logo]. Natural [warm/bright/clinical per template] lighting,
authentic UGC energy, realistic skin and material rendering, commercial advertising quality,
ultra photorealistic.
```
