# Prompt: Social Ads (Reels / TikTok / Stories)

**Use for:** vertical, thumb-stopping social ad creative.

## Direction

Generate a vertical 9:16 frame with the approved UGC model front and center, product/sticker clearly visible in the upper two-thirds of the frame (leaving lower third clear for platform UI and any caption overlay). Energy should be high and scroll-stopping.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio
- [ ] Model and sticker positioned to avoid platform UI overlap zones (bottom ~15%, top ~10%)
- [ ] Sticker legible even at small mobile thumbnail size — bold, high-contrast placement
- [ ] Passes `rules/quality_control.md`

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A vertical 9:16 social ad photograph/video frame. [Approved UGC model description] holds/uses
[product name] with high, scroll-stopping energy, positioned in the upper two-thirds of frame.
Sticker fully legible even at small size: [insert locked sticker analysis]. Background:
[insert from matching industry template]. Bright, punchy, mobile-optimized lighting and
contrast, authentic UGC style, ultra photorealistic.
```
