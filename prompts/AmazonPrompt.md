# Prompt: Amazon Listing Image

**Use for:** e-commerce marketplace main and secondary listing images.

## Direction

Generate a clean, marketplace-compliant product image: pure white or neutral background, product centered, sticker fully legible, no lifestyle clutter for the main image slot. Secondary images may reintroduce a hand/model per Amazon's supplementary image norms.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Pure white (or near-white) background for the main listing image
- [ ] Product fills 80-90% of frame, centered
- [ ] Sticker/label perfectly sharp and legible, zero distortion
- [ ] No added text/graphics on the main image (marketplace compliance)
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 1:1

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A clean e-commerce product photograph on a pure white background. [Product name] centered in
frame, filling approximately 85% of the image. The sticker/label is fully visible and
perfectly legible: [insert locked sticker analysis]. Studio lighting, no shadtype reflections
beyond a subtle natural contact shadow, no added graphics or text, marketplace-ready,
ultra photorealistic, 8K.
```
