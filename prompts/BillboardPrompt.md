# Prompt: Billboard / Large Format

**Use for:** billboards, large-format print, out-of-home advertising.

## Direction

Generate a bold, simplified composition designed to be legible from a distance. Fewer elements than a lifestyle shot — strong hero product/sticker, minimal background distraction, high contrast.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Composition reads clearly at a glance from far away — bold shapes, minimal clutter
- [ ] Sticker rendered at maximum legibility and scale-accuracy
- [ ] High contrast between subject and background
- [ ] Wide aspect ratio (16:9 or wider, matching the actual billboard dimensions if known)

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A bold, large-format outdoor advertising photograph, designed for maximum legibility at a
distance. [Product name] and [approved UGC model description] as the sole strong focal point,
minimal background elements, high contrast. Sticker rendered with maximum clarity and scale
accuracy: [insert locked sticker analysis]. Bright, punchy lighting, agency-grade billboard
advertising quality, ultra photorealistic, 8K.
```
