# Prompt: Shopify Banner

**Use for:** website hero banners, category headers, homepage feature images.

## Direction

Generate a wide-format banner image with the product and/or model composed to one side, leaving clean negative space on the opposite side for the store's own headline/CTA text to be overlaid later. Background follows the matching industry template but should be simplified so it doesn't compete with overlaid site copy.

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Clear negative space reserved on one third of the frame for text overlay
- [ ] Product and sticker remain sharp and legible in the occupied two-thirds
- [ ] Background simplified relative to a full lifestyle shot — supports, doesn't distract
- [ ] Aspect ratio: 16:9 (or per the store's theme requirements)

## Prompt Skeleton

```
A wide commercial banner photograph, [product name] and [approved UGC model description]
composed within the right two-thirds of the frame, left third left as clean, simplified
negative space for text overlay. Sticker fully legible: [insert locked sticker analysis].
Background: simplified version of [insert from matching industry template]. Bright, on-brand
lighting, ultra photorealistic, commercial web-banner quality.
```
